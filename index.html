THE POEM LUCY GRAY WAS WRITTEN BY WILLIAM. IT IS BASED ON A REAL INCIDENT THAT HAPPENED AT HALIFAX IN YORKSHIRE NARRATED TO HIM BY HIS SISTER DOROTHY. ACCORDING TO LOCAL LENGEND, LUCY'SPIRT COULD BE FELT IN THE REGION IN THE FORM OF A SUPERNATURAL APPARITTION THE REGION ALL ALONE AND SINGING CHEERGULLY.

THE POEM 'LUCY GRAY' STARTS WITH THE REFERENCE TO A POPULAR STORY OF LUCY GRAY. LUCY GRAY WAS AN INNOCENT CHILDWHO WAS GROWING UP IN THE COUNTRYSIDE OG INGLAND SHE DID NAT HAVE MANY NEIGHBOURS OT FRIENGS. SHE WAS RARELY SEEN OUTSIDE HER HOUSE.
